  • Daegu Metropolitan City, located in the center of Gyeongsang-do, is the fourth most populous metropolitan city in Korea. It is also a popular tourist destination due to its good accessibility as it has Daegu International Airport, KTX Dongdaegu Station, and Seodaegu Station. There are Daegu Modern Alley, where you can feel the footsteps of modern culture, Dongseong-ro, a vibrant young street, E-World and Duryu Park, which are good to go out with your family, and in summer, the city is rocked by the Chimaek Festival.
